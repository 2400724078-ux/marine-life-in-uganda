OUR MISSION & PURPOSE

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote awareness and understanding of Uganda's aquatic ecosystems, highlighting the importance of biodiversity and sustainability. We believe that education can inspire conservation and meaningful action that protects these vital water bodies for future generations.

Our Vision

We envision a Uganda where aquatic ecosystems are celebrated, protected, and sustainably managed — where communities, researchers, and governments work together to preserve the Pearl of Africa's extraordinary underwater heritage.

Economic Impact

ECONOMIC IMPORTANCE

Marine life in Uganda plays a vital role in the economy, supporting fisheries, tourism, and trade. The Nile Perch industry alone provides livelihoods to thousands across the Lake Victoria basin.

Uganda's fisheries sector contributes significantly to national GDP, with Lake Victoria being one of the most productive freshwater fisheries in the world.
